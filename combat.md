COMBAT
====

## Combat turn
* List of initiatives from highest to lowest
* Roll 1d20 + Accuracy value vs. Dodge value
* On hit:
   * Decapitation roll for zombies and chimeras (on a 20 roll)
   * Damage roll if the target isn't a zombie or if decaptiation didn't happen.

## Weapons
* Survival Knife `1d4`
* Samurai Edge `1d6`
* Shotgun `1d8`
* Grenade Launcher
  * Explosive rounds `1d10`
  * Acid rounds `1d12`
  * Flame rounds `1d8` (`1d12` on Plant 42, burns zombie bodies)
